Description

The Space

Beautiful, unique space, with 20' ceilings, exposed brick, and lovely lady roommates.Our guests are welcome to their room and the entire common area - kitchen, bathroom, the works!

If you like to cook we've got oodles of cool kitchen gadgets. If you like to drink, we've got all the bitters, shakers, muddlers and ice cubes you could ask for.We're all busy bees and spend relatively little daytime at home during the week.

In the evening one or two of us may be around, and we love to get to know our guests, but we're not intrusive, and happy to give you all the space you need. But if you'd like to join in for a beer or glass of wine, we'll be sure to offer you one!

Williamsburg is one stop from the big bad city, but a cozy little neighborhood all of its own.It's totally self sufficient, and a wonderful place to live and visit.Movie theatres, galleries, restaurants, bars, bowling allies, rooftops are all mere blocks away.The L train which will take you through the middle of Manahattan, or further east to Bushwick is a 5 minute walk.

House Rules

It's a non smoking apartment, but we're on the ground floor, so it's an easy walk to your nicotine fix!
